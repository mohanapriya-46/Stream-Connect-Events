Danger Zone

Irreversible actions that affect your account

Deactivate Account

Temporarily disable your account. Your profile and data will be hidden but not deleted. You can reactivate your account at any time by logging in again.

Delete Account

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including events, connections, messages, and payment history will be permanently deleted.

Clear All Data

Delete all your personal data from Stream Connect while keeping your account active. This includes event history, messages, connections, and preferences.

Important Notice
Before taking any irreversible actions, consider:
  • Exporting your data first (use the "Export Data" button at the top)
  • Contacting support if you're experiencing issues
  • Checking if deactivation solves your concern instead of deletion
