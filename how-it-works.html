Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do I need?

Basic requirements: computer/laptop, webcam, microphone, and stable internet. We recommend a dedicated microphone and good lighting for professional quality.

How do I monetize events?

Choose from ticket sales, subscriptions, pay-per-view, donations, or sponsorships. All payments are processed securely with automatic payouts.

Is technical support available?

Yes! We offer 24/7 chat support, email support, and dedicated account managers for premium plans. Plus extensive documentation and video tutorials.

Can I record events?

All plans include event recording. Recordings are automatically saved in your dashboard and can be downloaded or shared directly with attendees.

Are there mobile apps?

Yes! We have native iOS and Android apps for both organizers and attendees with full feature parity for on-the-go event management.

Can I test before committing?

Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start your trial.
